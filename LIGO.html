LIGO and ground-based detectors

We analyze data from the ground-based LIGO detectors to infer the properties of merging black holes and neutron stars and contribute to papers prepared by the LIGO/Virgo/KAGRA collaborations.

We develop new methods to extract more information from gravitational wave strain data and study the impact of data-quality issues, noise non-stationarity, overlapping sources, and in general any situation that might violate our assumptions.
