LISA

We explore and construct data analysis methods for the upcoming space-based LISA detector which will observe gravitational waves in the milliHertz regime. The LISA data will simultaneously contain massive black hole binaries, extreme mass ratio inspirals, millions of galactic white dwarf binaries, and many more. Toward preparation for the signal-rich LISA data and the unique challenges they pose, we are organizing the second LISA Sprint at Caltech in April 2024.
